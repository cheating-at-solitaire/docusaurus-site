# Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password or it has expired, follow these steps.

## Option 1 - Forgotten Password

1. Open the login page.
2. Select **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Select **Submit**.
5. Check your email for the reset link.
6. Create a new password.

## Creating a Strong Password

Your password should contain:

- At least 12 characters
- Uppercase letters
- Lowercase letters
- Numbers
- Special characters

Example:

```
Winter2026!Coffee
```

Do not reuse passwords from other websites.

## Password Expired

If your password has expired:

1. Log in using your old password.
2. Follow the prompts to create a new password.

## Common Problems

### Reset Email Not Received

Check:

- Junk Mail
- Spam Folder
- Deleted Items

Wait up to 15 minutes before requesting another email.

### Reset Link Has Expired

Simply request another password reset.

### Account Locked

If your account becomes locked, contact the IT Service Desk.

## Need More Help?

Provide:

- Your username
- The email address registered on your account
- Any error messages displayed
