# Logging into the Website

This guide explains how to access the company website.

## Before You Begin

Ensure you have:

- A valid username or email address
- Your password
- Internet access
- A supported web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari)

## Step 1 - Open the Website

Open your web browser and navigate to:

```
https://yourcompanywebsite.com
```

## Step 2 - Enter Your Credentials

1. Enter your username or email address.
2. Enter your password.
3. Click **Sign In**.

## Step 3 - Complete Multi-Factor Authentication (If Enabled)

If prompted:

1. Open your authentication app.
2. Enter the verification code.
3. Select **Verify**.

## Troubleshooting

### Incorrect Username or Password

- Check Caps Lock is off.
- Ensure your email address is entered correctly.
- Try typing your password manually.

### Account Locked

After several failed login attempts, your account may become locked.

Contact the IT Service Desk if this happens.

### Website Not Loading

Try the following:

- Refresh the page.
- Try another browser.
- Restart your computer.
- Check your internet connection.

## Need More Help?

Contact the IT Service Desk with:

- Your username
- A screenshot of any error message
- The time the issue occurred
